As I straddle the responsibilities of school, work, and personal interests, I am driven by a resolute commitment to continuous learning and the unwavering pursuit of a future defined by innovation and personal fulfillment.

My Skills

Over the years, my skill set has undergone significant growth. My journey started in third grade when I delved into the world of programming, with Python as my initial canvas. Back then, my creations were simple programs crafting intricate shapes and patterns. Fast forward to today, Python has evolved into the programming language I am most adept at. I've progressed beyond drawing shapes to crafting advanced programs; one notable project involves web scraping weather data and presenting it in an ordered list, ready for versatile applications.

Several years ago, I acquired an affordable 3D printer, sparking my fascination with customization. I've invested time and effort in modifying and maintaining it to a point where I can trust it to reliably bring my designs to life. The 3D printing journey prompted me to explore the world of 3D modeling, leading to a two-year deep dive into the intricacies of Fusion 360. I've dedicated countless hours honing my skills, modeling anything from functional replacements—like a part for a broken music stand—to more intricate designs that cater to various needs. This evolution showcases not only my technical proficiency but also my commitment to expanding my capabilities in the ever-evolving landscape of technology and creativity.
